Realtime Whiteboard

Realtime whiteboard is an online hangout space with features similar to OneNote and Photoshop made for people that just want to hang out and plan their next outting. It's fun, social, and interactive.

Features

  • Syncs in realtime!! Chat, draw, livestream, even upload gifs. Everything is a block that you can move around and this updates live for all clients in the room !!
  • Meet up with all your friends in a room and updates will be saved in our database for next time.
  • Say more with visual tools
  • Livestream video using your phone or webcam
  • Send live messages in chatroom
  • Share laughs and memories with stickers, gifs, images, avatars, and more.

My responsibilities: I did all of the Backend and Angular work, most of the app. My partner did the html templates and css for most of the app.

Angular Firebase Bootstrap Angular Material

A Restaurant Service App Similar to SkipTheDishes

I did this for my midterm at Lighthouse Labs in a team of 3. Lighthouse eats, an app similar to SkipTheDishes and other food delivery apps. It has route guards and basic authentication enabling user-stories. A customer can login and visit all the restaurants available on our list. Clicking a store gives you access to restaurants menu and cart. On checkout we provide a basic estimated wait time with animation. The app doesn't end here. The restaurant owner's user story starts. After an order has been processed, a logged in store owner can visit his restaurants backend and manage the order in an interactive way.

My responsibilities: I did the entire backend that includes PostgresSQL, Knex, and Express.In addition, I did some front end such as the login page, the login component, and the checkout animation timer.
